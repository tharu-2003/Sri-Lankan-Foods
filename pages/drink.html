Sri Lankan Drinks

Tea

Sri Lanka, formerly known as Ceylon, is renowned for its tea production, particularly "Ceylon Tea," a world-famous brand.

Belimal

In Sri Lanka, "Belimal" refers to a popular herbal tea made from the Bael flower, commonly known as Beli Mal in Sinhala.

King coconut

King coconut is a specific variety of coconut native to Sri Lanka, known for its bright orange shell and sweeter, more delicate water

King Coconut Drink (Thambili)

King Coconut (also called **Thambili**) is a popular and refreshing drink in Sri Lanka. The water inside the King Coconut is naturally sweet, and it's packed with electrolytes, making it an excellent thirst quencher and a natural hydrating drink. It's perfect for hot days or when you're feeling dehydrated.

Ingredients

  • 1 King Coconut (Thambili)
  • Optional: Ice cubes (if serving chilled)
  • Optional: A pinch of salt or sugar (for added taste)
  • Optional: A few drops of lime or lemon juice (for extra freshness)

Instructions

  1. Use a sharp knife or coconut opener to pierce the top of the King Coconut to access the water.
  2. Pour the coconut water into a glass. If desired, strain the water to remove any bits of coconut flesh.
  3. If you prefer chilled coconut water, add ice cubes to the glass or refrigerate the water beforehand.
  4. For extra flavor, you can add a pinch of salt, sugar, or a few drops of lime/lemon juice.
  5. Stir well and enjoy the fresh, natural hydration!

Health Benefits

  • Rich in electrolytes such as potassium, sodium, and magnesium, making it an excellent hydrating drink.
  • Helps to replenish lost fluids and is ideal for rehydration after exercise or during hot weather.
  • Contains antioxidants that help improve skin health and protect against free radicals.
  • Promotes digestion and boosts the immune system due to its natural antibacterial properties.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve chilled with a slice of lime for added zest.
  • Pair with a light meal or enjoy as a refreshing snack during hot weather.

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

  • Calories: ~45-60 kcal (for 1 cup of coconut water)
  • Carbohydrates: 9-15g
  • Electrolytes: High (especially potassium)
  • Fats: Negligible

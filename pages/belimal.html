Sri Lankan Belimal (Beli Mal) Tea

Belimal Tea is a traditional herbal tea in Sri Lanka made from the dried flowers of the **Bael (Beli)** fruit. It is widely consumed for its health benefits, including its ability to soothe digestion, reduce inflammation, and strengthen the immune system. This tea has a unique, slightly bitter, and aromatic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 dried Bael (Beli) flowers
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar (optional, depending on taste)
  • 1-2 slices of lemon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil in a saucepan or kettle.
  2. Add the dried Bael (Beli) flowers to the boiling water.
  3. Allow the flowers to steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on how strong you want the flavor.
  4. Once steeped, strain the tea to remove the flower pieces.
  5. If desired, add honey or sugar for sweetness, and a slice of lemon for extra freshness.
  6. Pour the tea into a cup, and enjoy a refreshing cup of Belimal tea!

Health Benefits

  • Helps to soothe the digestive system and relieve constipation.
  • Acts as a natural anti-inflammatory and may help reduce body aches.
  • Boosts the immune system and helps prevent common colds.
  • Has antioxidant properties that help fight free radicals in the body.
  • Helps in calming the mind and promoting relaxation.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve hot for a soothing drink, especially after meals to aid digestion.
  • For an added boost of flavor, add a pinch of cinnamon or a sprig of mint.

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

  • Calories: ~2 kcal (without honey or sugar)
  • Antioxidants: High
  • Vitamins: Contains Vitamin C and other essential nutrients

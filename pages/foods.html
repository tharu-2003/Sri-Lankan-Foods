Sri Lankan Foods

Kottu roti

Kottu roti is a popular Sri Lankan dish made by stir-frying chopped roti (flatbread) with vegetables, scrambled eggs, and often meat or seafood

String hoppers

String hoppers are a traditional Sri Lankan breakfast staple made from thin, steamed rice flour "noodles"

Hoppers

Hoppers are a popular Sri Lankan pancake made with fermented rice flour batter and coconut milk
